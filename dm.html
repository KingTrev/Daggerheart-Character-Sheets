DICE & ROLLS ⇔
ACTION ROLL
1. Pick a trait · 2. GM sets Difficulty (default 12) · 3. Declare modifiers · 4. Roll Hope Die + Fear Die + trait · 5. Resolve — something always changes
DUALITY DICE RESULTS
CRITICALDice match — auto-succeed regardless of Difficulty. Gain Hope, clear Stress. Attack = add max dice result to damage.
SUCCESS / HOPEMeets Difficulty, Hope Die higher. Succeed + gain 1 Hope.
SUCCESS / FEARMeets Difficulty, Fear Die higher. Succeed, GM gains 1 Fear.
FAIL / HOPEBelow Difficulty, Hope Die higher. Fail + minor consequence + gain 1 Hope.
FAIL / FEARBelow Difficulty, Fear Die higher. Fail + major consequence, GM gains 1 Fear.
ATTACK & DAMAGE
Attack: Roll vs target Evasion (PCs) or Difficulty score (adversaries). Unarmed = Strength or Finesse, d4+Prof physical.
On hit: Roll damage + Proficiency. Compare total to thresholds:
MINOR
Mark 1 HP
MAJOR
Mark 2 HP
SEVERE
Mark 3 HP · Crit = add max possible dice to damage roll
Armor Slot: Before marking HP, mark a slot to reduce severity by one threshold.
OTHER ROLL TYPES
Reaction Roll: Dodge/resist something. Like action roll but no Hope/Fear generated, no GM moves triggered. Crit = ignore effect entirely.
Group Action: One leader rolls action. Others make reaction rolls. Leader gets +1 per success, −1 per failure from collaborators.
Tag Team: Once/session per PC. Spend 3 Hope. Both roll — choose which result to use. Both roll damage and add totals on hit.

CONDITIONS
VULNERABLEAll rolls targeting them have advantage.
RESTRAINEDCan't move. Can still take actions.
HIDDENRolls against have disadvantage. Ends on attack or line of sight.
Conditions can't stack. Clear with action roll (PC) or spend Fear to spotlight adversary (GM).
DEATH MOVES
BLAZE OF GLORYOne final auto-crit action, then cross the veil.
AVOID DEATHFall unconscious. Roll Hope Die — if result ≤ level, gain a scar (cross out a Hope slot). Return when ally heals 1 HP or after long rest.
RISK IT ALLRoll Duality Dice. Hope higher = stay up, clear HP/Stress = Hope Die value. Fear higher = die. Match = survive, clear all.
DOWNTIME
Short Rest — 2 moves: Tend Wounds (1d4+Tier HP), Clear Stress (1d4+Tier), Repair Armor (1d4+Tier slots), Prepare (+Hope). GM gains 1d4 Fear.
Long Rest — 2 moves: Same as above but clear ALL. Swap domain cards. GM gains 1d4+party size Fear. Advance a long countdown.
After 3 short rests in a row, next must be a long rest.
GM MOVES ⇔
FEAR & HOPE
Start: 1 Fear per PC. Max 12. Carries over between sessions.
Gain Fear when: PC rolls with Fear · short rest (1d4) · long rest (1d4 + PCs) · certain abilities
Spend Fear to: Steal spotlight · Make a GM move · Use adversary Fear Feature · Use environment Fear Feature · Add adversary Experience to a roll
Hope max: 6 per PC. Start with 2. Carries over.
Players spend Hope to: Add Experience (+2) · Help ally (d6 advantage die) · Activate Hope Feature (3 Hope) · Tag Team (3 Hope) · Recall domain card
WHEN TO MAKE A GM MOVE
• PC rolls with Fear
• PC fails an action roll
• Action has unavoidable consequences
• PC gives you a golden opportunity
• Players look to you for what happens next
CRITGive additional opportunity or advantage
SUCCESS/HOPEShow how the world reacts — soft move
SUCCESS/FEARComplication follows success — medium move
FAIL/FEARHard move — direct, no opening to interrupt
GM MOVE LIST

GM PRINCIPLES
• Begin and end with the fiction
• Collaborate at all times, especially in conflict
• Fill the world with life, wonder, and danger
• Ask questions and incorporate the answers
• Make every roll important
• Play to find out what happens
• Hold on gently — prep situations, not scripts
Best Practices: Cultivate a curious table · Gain trust · Keep story moving · Cut to the action · Tell them what they'd know · Ground the world in motive · Reframe rather than reject
