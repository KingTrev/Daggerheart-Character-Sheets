DICE & ROLLS ⇔
ACTION ROLL — STEP BY STEP
1. Announce action + trait
2. GM sets Difficulty
3. Roll 2d12 (Hope Die + Fear Die) + Trait modifier
4. Compare total to Difficulty
5. Announce result with Hope or Fear
DUALITY DICE RESULTS
✦ SUCCESS WITH HOPE
Total ≥ Difficulty, Hope Die higher. Succeed + gain 1 Hope.
✧ SUCCESS WITH FEAR
Total ≥ Difficulty, Fear Die higher. Succeed, GM gains 1 Fear.
✦ FAILURE WITH HOPE
Total < Difficulty, Hope Die higher. Fail + minor consequence + gain 1 Hope.
✧ FAILURE WITH FEAR
Total < Difficulty, Fear Die higher. Fail + major consequence, GM gains 1 Fear.
Critical Success: Matching dice — auto-succeed with bonus, gain Hope, clear Stress. Attack = add max possible dice result to damage.
REACTION ROLL
Response to an attack or hazard. Works like action roll but does not generate Hope or Fear and doesn't trigger GM moves. Critical success ignores effects but no Hope/Stress benefit.
GROUP ACTION ROLL
One PC leads (action roll), others make reaction rolls. Lead gets +1 per reaction success, −1 per reaction failure.
TAG TEAM ROLL
Once per session per PC — spend 3 Hope to initiate. Both PCs roll, choose which result applies. On Hope: all gain Hope. On Fear: GM gains Fear per PC involved. Both roll damage on hit, add totals.

PROFICIENCY & DAMAGE
Proficiency = number of damage dice. Tier 1: 1 die · Tier 2: 1 die · Tier 3: 2 dice · Tier 4: 3 dice
Unarmed: [Proficiency]d4 physical. Strength or Finesse (GM's choice).
Critical damage: Roll damage normally, then add the maximum possible result of the damage dice to the total.

PLAYER MECHANICS ⇔
EVASION
Attack rolls against a PC use their Evasion as the Difficulty. Set by class + equipment + conditions. Against adversaries, attacks use the adversary's Difficulty.
HIT POINTS & DAMAGE THRESHOLDS
Thresholds = armor base thresholds + PC level.
MINORBelow Major threshold → mark 1 HP
MAJORAt/above Major, below Severe → mark 2 HP
SEVEREAt/above Severe → mark 3 HP
Damage reduced to 0 or below: no HP marked. When last HP marked: make a death move.
ARMOR SLOTS
Mark 1 Armor Slot to reduce HP marked by 1. Armor Score = base + bonuses. Armor Score of 0 = can't mark Armor Slots.
STRESS
When last Stress marked: become Vulnerable until at least 1 is cleared. When must mark Stress but can't: mark HP instead. Max Stress set by class, typically 6.
HELPING AN ALLY
Spend 1 Hope to help. They gain +1 to their Hope or Fear Die result. Multiple PCs can help the same roll.
CONDITIONS
HIDDENRolls against them have disadvantage. Ends when seen or when they attack.
RESTRAINEDCan't move. Can still act from current position.
VULNERABLEAll rolls targeting them have advantage.
Clearing temp conditions (PC): Make an action roll. (Adversary): GM spends their spotlight — no roll, but uses the adversary's action. Spend 1 Fear to end a temporary effect.

DEATH MOVES
BLAZE OF GLORYEmbrace death. One last heroic act — then cross the veil.
AVOID DEATHSurvive with consequences. Roll Hope Die: on a 1, gain a scar (cross out a Hope slot). Cross out last Hope slot: journey ends.
RISK IT ALLRoll Duality Dice. Hope higher: survive, clear HP/Stress = Hope Die value. Fear higher: cross the veil. Tie: clear all HP and Stress.
DOWNTIME MOVES
Short rest — 2 moves: Tend to Wounds (clear 1d4+Tier HP), Clear Stress (1d4+Tier), Repair Armor (1d4+Tier slots), Prepare (Hope + advantage on one roll), Work on Project. GM gains 1d4 Fear.
Long rest — 2 moves: Tend All Wounds, Clear All Stress, Repair All Armor, Prepare (2 Hope each), Work on Project. GM gains 1d4+PCs Fear + may advance a long-term countdown.

GM TOOLS ⇔
HOPE — PLAYERS SPEND TO
• Add an Experience bonus to a roll
• Help an ally (+1 to die result)
• Initiate a Tag Team Roll (3 Hope)
• Activate a Hope Feature (usually 3 Hope)
• Recall a spent domain card
Max 6 Hope per PC. Carries over between sessions.
FEAR — GM SPENDS TO
• Steal spotlight & make a GM move
• Make an additional GM move
• Use an adversary's Fear Feature
• Use an environment's Fear Feature
• Add adversary Experience to a roll
• End a temporary condition or effect
Max 12 Fear. Start with 1 Fear per PC. Gain Fear when PCs roll with Fear or rest.
GM MOVES — WHEN & HOW
Make a move when a PC rolls with Fear or fails an action roll.
SUCCESS / HOPEYes, and… Player succeeds, gains Hope.
SUCCESS / FEARYes, but… Succeeds, then consequence. GM gains Fear.
FAILURE / HOPENo, but… Fails + minor consequence, gains Hope.
FAILURE / FEARNo, and… Fails + major consequence, GM gains Fear.
EXAMPLE GM MOVES
Have a PC mark Stress · Separate the party · Introduce a new threat · Reveal an unwelcome truth · Show collateral damage · Clear an adversary's condition · Spotlight an adversary · Trigger an environment effect · Advance a countdown · Put someone in a tough spot
Softer moves on Hope results, harder moves on Fear results.

IMPROVISING ADVERSARIES
Give them a Difficulty and a name. Base moves on their motives. They don't need stat blocks — just put spotlight on what they do and show how the world reacts. Spend Fear to escalate.
GM PRINCIPLES
• Portray a rich and dangerous world
• Fill the world with interesting characters
• Play to find out what happens
• Address the characters, not the players
• Embrace failure as a story tool
• Telegraph danger before it strikes
• Be a fan of the player characters
• Spend Fear — don't hoard it
• Make the world feel consequential
